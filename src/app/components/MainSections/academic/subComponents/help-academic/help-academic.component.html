Hello! Welcome to our little guide

This section is dedicated for your academic tracking. In the Home section you can see what classes you have left for the day/week alongside their tasks. On the Schedule section you can see all your classes and the days of the week where they are assigned. Here you can manage them and also import a new schedule

Where?

Its quite easy! By clicking the Import Schedule Button and by entering your group name, the id of the google sheet and by selecting your school year

How do I get the id?

https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/spreadsheetId/edit#gid=0

Why am I getting error messages?

If you encounter the message Duplicate classes it means you have to clear the schedule first. You can easily do this by using the Clear Schedule Button. If you're enountering some other type of error it means that either the id you provided is not valid or the group doesn't exist.

You can create projects for all the laboratories or seminars. In the Projects section you can create those said projects where you see their percentage of completion. This percentage is calculated using the finished parent tasks from the Tasks section

Once you have a project created, here you can create and manage it's tasks. By clicking on a task it will expand itself and show its current subtasks and give you more details by showing the Description field. By hovering the task body the possible actions will show: adding a child task, modifying the tasks or deleting it
