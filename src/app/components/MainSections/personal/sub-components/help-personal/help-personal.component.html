Hello! Welcome to our little guide

This section is dedicated for your free time and hobbies. Here you can manage personal tasks and keep track of your passions like video games, books or even movies!

Where?

All your tasks are organized in multiple columns which make a single board! You can create as many of them as you wish. You can also use the labels to give your tasks more color and more intel about their importance.

Those lists are meant for you to track your progress! Here you can separate each entry by its status. For example Reading/Read/Plan To Read/Dropped for books. The other lists follow a similar structure. You can also sort them based on their Rating by clicking on the Rating label!

Yes! You can export each of your lists in a PDF file! This way you can make sure you save your progress and share it with your friends!
